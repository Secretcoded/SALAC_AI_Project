How Can We Help You?

Find answers to commonly asked questions about maniEd's features, security, payments, and our AI assistant Edibot.

How do I create a maniEd account?

Creating a maniEd account is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  1. Download the maniEd app from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Click "Sign Up" and enter your email address
  3. Verify your email with the code we send you
  4. Set up your profile and security preferences
  5. Add a payment method to start using your account

You'll need to provide a valid government-issued ID for verification purposes, as required by financial regulations.

What documents do I need to verify my account?

To comply with financial regulations and ensure account security, we require the following documents:

  • Government-issued photo ID: Passport, driver's license, or national ID card
  • Proof of address: Recent utility bill, bank statement, or government document (within last 3 months)
  • Selfie verification: A clear photo of yourself holding your ID

Document verification typically takes 1-2 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your account is fully verified.

How long do transfers take?

Transfer times vary depending on the type of transaction and destination:

  • Domestic transfers: Instant to 1 business day
  • International transfers: 1-3 business days
  • Card-to-card transfers: Instant
  • Bank transfers: 1-2 business days

Some transfers may be subject to additional security checks, which can extend processing times. You'll always be notified of your transfer status via email and push notifications.

What are the fees for sending money?

maniEd offers competitive and transparent pricing with no hidden fees:

  • Domestic transfers: Free for personal accounts
  • International transfers: 0.5-2% of transfer amount (varies by country)
  • Currency conversion: Real-time exchange rates with a small markup
  • Business accounts: Competitive rates starting at 0.3%

All fees are displayed clearly before you confirm any transaction. You can view detailed fee information in our pricing section or contact support for specific quotes.

Can I cancel a transfer after sending it?

Transfer cancellation depends on the status of your transaction:

  • Pending transfers: Can be cancelled within 30 minutes of initiation
  • Processing transfers: Limited cancellation options - contact support immediately
  • Completed transfers: Cannot be cancelled, but you can request a recall

To cancel a pending transfer, go to your transaction history and select the transfer. If cancellation is available, you'll see a "Cancel" button. Some transfers may not be cancellable due to regulatory requirements or processing status.

How secure is my money with maniEd?

maniEd employs multiple layers of security to protect your funds and personal information:

  • Bank-level encryption: 256-bit AES encryption for all data
  • Biometric authentication: Fingerprint and facial recognition support
  • Two-factor authentication: SMS, email, and authenticator app options
  • Real-time monitoring: 24/7 fraud detection and prevention
  • Regulatory compliance: Licensed and regulated in all operating countries

Additionally, customer funds are held in segregated accounts and are protected by financial compensation schemes where applicable. We never store your full card details and use tokenization for all payment information.

What should I do if I suspect fraudulent activity?

If you notice any suspicious activity on your account, take immediate action:

  1. Freeze your account: Use the emergency freeze feature in the app
  2. Change passwords: Update your maniEd password and any linked email passwords
  3. Review transactions: Check your recent activity for unauthorized transactions
  4. Contact support: Report the issue immediately via the app or website
  5. Document everything: Take screenshots and note any relevant details

Our fraud team is available 24/7 to investigate suspicious activity. We'll work with you to secure your account and reverse any unauthorized transactions where possible. Contact us at security@manied-protect.com for urgent security issues.

What can Edibot help me with?

Edibot is your AI-powered financial assistant that can help with various tasks:

  • Account management: Check balances, view transaction history, update profile information
  • Payment assistance: Guide you through sending money, setting up recurring payments
  • Financial insights: Analyze spending patterns, suggest budget optimizations
  • Security support: Help with account verification, security settings, fraud concerns
  • Feature explanations: Explain how to use different maniEd features

Edibot is available 24/7 and can understand natural language questions. Simply type your question or choose from suggested topics to get instant assistance.

Is my data secure when using Edibot?

Yes, your data is completely secure when interacting with Edibot:

  • Encrypted communications: All conversations are encrypted end-to-end
  • Limited data access: Edibot only accesses information necessary to assist you
  • No data retention: Conversation history is not stored permanently
  • Privacy compliance: Fully compliant with GDPR and other privacy regulations
  • Anonymous analytics: Only anonymized usage data is collected for improvement

Edibot cannot perform sensitive actions like sending money without your explicit confirmation. For security-critical operations, you'll always be redirected to secure authentication flows.

How do I update my account information?

You can update most account information directly through the app or website:

  • Personal details: Name, email, phone number - available in Settings > Profile
  • Address: Can be updated in Settings > Personal Information
  • Payment methods: Add, remove, or update cards and bank accounts
  • Security settings: Password, 2FA, biometric settings

For security reasons, some changes (like legal name changes) may require additional verification and supporting documents. Contact support if you need help with sensitive account updates.

Limits can be increased based on your usage history and additional verification. Contact support to request higher limits for business or high-volume personal use.

Is maniEd available in my country?

maniEd is currently available in over 50 countries across North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America. We're constantly expanding our global presence.

Major markets include: United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Australia, Japan, Singapore, Brazil, Mexico, and many more.

To check if maniEd is available in your country, simply download the app and enter your location during signup. If we're not yet available, you can join our waitlist to be notified when we launch in your region.

How does maniEd protect against fraud?

maniEd uses advanced fraud prevention systems to protect your account:

  • Machine learning algorithms: Analyze transaction patterns to detect anomalies
  • Real-time monitoring: 24/7 surveillance of all account activity
  • Behavioral biometrics: Analyze how you interact with the app to verify identity
  • Device fingerprinting: Identify suspicious devices and locations
  • Transaction limits: Automatic limits that adapt based on your usage patterns

If we detect suspicious activity, we may temporarily freeze your account and contact you for verification. This is for your protection and helps prevent unauthorized access to your funds.

Can Edibot help me with investment advice?

Edibot can provide general financial insights and educational information, but cannot offer personalized investment advice:

Important: Edibot is not a licensed financial advisor. Any information provided is for educational purposes only and should not be considered as investment advice.

What Edibot CAN help with:

  • Budget tracking and spending analysis
  • General financial education and tips
  • Explaining financial concepts
  • Helping you understand your spending patterns

For personalized investment advice, please consult with a qualified financial advisor. Edibot is designed to help you manage your day-to-day finances and make informed decisions about your money.

How do I close my maniEd account?

To close your maniEd account, follow these steps:

  1. Withdraw any remaining balance from your account
  2. Cancel any recurring payments or subscriptions
  3. Download your transaction history for your records
  4. Contact customer support to request account closure
  5. Complete the account closure verification process

Important considerations:

  • Account closure is permanent and cannot be reversed
  • You'll lose access to your transaction history after 30 days
  • Any pending transactions must be completed before closure
  • Business accounts may require additional verification steps

If you're experiencing issues with your account, we recommend contacting support first as we may be able to resolve any problems without closing your account.
